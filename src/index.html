GDG Glasgow Events

What is GDG Glasgow?

Google Developers Group (GDG) is an open and volunteer-run geek community that create exciting projects and share experience about Google technologies with a passion. GDG Glasgow is a group of developers aiming to manipulate technological advancements under Google's technological guidance. We do this in the form of events that encourage everyone to participate as well as being open and having a nurturing environment. Our goal is to organize space to connect the best industry experts with the city of Glasgow.

What kind of events are hosted?

Evening Talk

Evening talks are short 2-3 hour talks where a small group of people get together in a local office and listen to some speakers present their topics as well as network and enjoy light refreshments.

Study Jam

Study Jams are community-run study groups for developers of all skill levels on topics such as Android, Google Cloud, TensorFlow, and other Google technologies. The objective of these study groups is to raise the technical proficiency of our members as well as to have fun while learning exciting new technologies.

DevFest

GDG DevFests are large tech conferences held in Glasgow. They are full of speakers and workshops all presented by the world's experts. Everyone is encouraged to attend. DevFests are usually one or two days long.

With many more planned!
